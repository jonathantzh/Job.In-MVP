Airflink | How it began

It began with a vision: to eliminate the unmet expectations of students seeking employment.

Soon, the team realised that Singapore's SMEs and Start-Ups had so much to offer, but were consistently outmarketed by large Multi-National Corporations. Worse, many new hires did not stay for long.

The result: Airflink. We aim to attract talent to enable SMEs and Start-Ups to grow sustainably with reduced attrition, via building powerful relationships between them and employees.

SSR | What it is

Smart Social Recruiting: Leveraging on relationships between peers and prospective employers to create the ideal job-fit.

Smart Social Recruiting is primarily executed in 3 phases: enhancing an employer's branding, building social connections and facilitating communication within the recruiting network.

The end goal: Building a powerful relationship between job applicants and prospective employers.

Recruitment | How can we help your Company

The Airflink platform seeks to develop a talent sharing community built on the principles of raising employer branding and facilitating simple recruiting.

1. Create a company profile and collect all job applicants' video-based personal branding marketing and online business cards in one platform, making it much easier for you to search and find their individual profiles.

2. Advertise your companies to relevant candidates, clients or target groups that you specify. Provide your candidates with information with respect to the company and job nature through virtual job tours and simple corporate videos. Make your company stand out in the eyes of prospective job candidates, by producing virtual job tours that showcase more about your company.

3. Watch your candidates perform, view their personalities and get to know them better before inviting them to the interview. Get an early impression of your job applicants and ease the recruiting process.

And the best of it all: It's free to join!
